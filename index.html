About FullwayMarine

福爾威設立於2022年，由數位具備實務操作的船長與輪機長所組成的團隊，對於船舶管理擁有豐富的經驗。經管理的船舶有客船、冷凍船、離岸風電船、油輪，提供ISM/ISPS國際法規下協助船東證書管理，船員培訓、管理以及招募，船用備件供應、機務服務與塢修技術指導，船舶檢驗與換證具備多年經驗，可提供船東買賣船舶與船舶拆解服務。

Since 2022, Fullwaymarine has assembled an exceptional team comprising several captains and chief engineers with extensive practical experience. We manage various types of vessels, including passage ships, reefer ships, offshore vessels, and oil tankers. Our services include compliance with ISM and ISPS regulations to assist ship owners with all certificate maintenance. Additionally, we offer crew training, recruitment, and interview services. The company also provides technical instruction, consulting, surveying, and spare parts supply. Therefore, any mortgage sales of ships, scrapping, or new shipbuilding supervision can find solutions and consulting services with us.

塢修/Docking Repair

塢修監造,船員可以瞭解船舶在航期間不能查看的區域內的實際情況。它還可以幫助船舶管理人對船體和船舶在水中時不能接觸到的設備狀況進行評估。

During docking, the crew can gain insight into the actual conditions in areas that are not visible while the ship is underway. This process also assists ship managers in evaluating the condition of the hull and equipment that are inaccessible while the ship is in the water.

船舶管理/Ship Management

船舶管理,海上交通監督管理的重要內容，包括船舶登記管理、船舶進出港口簽證管理、外國籍船舶管理、船舶機務/技術管理以及船舶離靠碼頭管理等。

Ship management plays a crucial role in the supervision and management of maritime traffic. This encompasses various aspects, including ship registration, port entry and exit visa management, foreign ship oversight, maintenance or technical management, and the coordination of departures and dockings.

船員管理/Crew Management

配員服務,船員管理,依據客戶需求招募國籍與外籍優質船員、提供船員派遣服務以及船員保險等。

Manning services include crew management, recruiting high-quality national and foreign crews based on customer needs, providing crew dispatch services, and offering crew insurance, among other services.
